## 📋 Complete Job Description
A full-stack software engineering role at a mission-driven EdTech company, focused on product ownership rather than ticket execution. The engineer will work end-to-end on features — from understanding customer pain points through direct conversations, to architecture, implementation, and deployment. The role sits within a team of 8 engineers (5 senior, 3 junior) led by an experienced Head of Engineering and Principal Engineer, operating across several time zones with a Melbourne-based hybrid arrangement.

---

## ✅ Requirements

### Essential:
- 3–5 years of professional software engineering experience
- Strong frontend skills with React and TypeScript
- Solid understanding of reactive data flow, state management, and GraphQL consumption
- Backend experience (Elixir preferred but not required — willingness to learn is key)
- Ability to reason confidently about data structures, type systems, APIs, and concurrency
- Product mindset — naturally curious about users, asks "why" before "how"
- Comfort with ambiguity — able to turn vague problems into shipped solutions
- Strong communication skills for customer conversations and team collaboration

### Desirable:
- Experience with Elixir and GraphQL on the backend
- Personal projects, open-source contributions, or active tinkering/enthusiasm for the craft
- Experience making architectural trade-off decisions (UX, performance, scalability, business impact)
