## 📋 Complete Job Description
An entry-level digital marketing coordinator role designed for someone at the very beginning of their career. The successful candidate will support the planning and execution of digital campaigns across multiple channels including social, search, email, and websites. The role offers broad exposure to how digital marketing works day-to-day, working alongside experienced marketers, creatives, and partners. Flaunt also provides a unique Discovery Days process and pairs every successful candidate with an industry coach for the first six months.
