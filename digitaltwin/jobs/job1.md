## 📋 Complete Job Description
A rotational graduate program designed for tech-focused graduates, placing them at the forefront of innovation using AI and leading-edge technology. Graduates will work on real-life projects from day one, supported by mentors, leaders, and program managers. The program includes hackathons, a Graduate-run Technology Committee, external certifications, and industry-leading events.

---

## ✅ Requirements

### Essential:
- Permanent Resident or Citizen of Australia or New Zealand at time of application *(excludes bridging visas/820 visa holders)*
- In final year of study OR completed degree within the last 24 months
- Minimum **70 WAM** for Engineering / Cyber Security streams
- Minimum **65 WAM** for Data Science stream
- Based in Sydney or willing to relocate for the program

### Desirable:
- Passion for innovation and eagerness to learn new tech stacks, languages, and frameworks
- Curious problem-solver who enjoys simplifying processes
- Customer-focused mindset
