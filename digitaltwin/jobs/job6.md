## 📋 Complete Job Description
Accenture Australia is seeking a Junior Data Analyst to join its growing Data & AI consulting team. This entry-level role is ideal for graduates or early-career professionals interested in data analytics, business intelligence, and technology consulting.

The successful candidate will assist in collecting, cleaning, and analysing business data to support strategic decision-making and digital transformation initiatives. You will collaborate with consultants, engineers, and stakeholders while gaining hands-on experience working with enterprise datasets, dashboards, and reporting tools.

---

## ✅ Requirements

### Essential:
- Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology, Data Analytics, Computer Science, Business Analytics, or related field
- Basic understanding of data analysis concepts
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Good communication and teamwork abilities
- Willingness to learn new tools and technologies

### Desirable:
- Familiarity with SQL, Python, or R
- Experience with Power BI, Tableau, or other data visualization tools
- Academic or internship projects involving data analysis
- Understanding of databases or statistics fundamentals

### Not Required:
- Prior industry experience *(graduate-level role)*
- Advanced programming knowledge *(training provided)*
