## SLIDE 5 — Collaboration Tools
**Presenter:** Pablo | **Duration:** 1 minute

-For our technology stack, we divided our tools into collaboration tools and core technologies.
-For collaboration, we relied on several platforms to keep our team organized and working efficiently throughout the project.
-We used ClickUp to manage tasks, assign responsibilities, and monitor our overall progress. This helped us stay aligned with deadlines and clearly understand each member’s role at every stage of development.
-For development collaboration, we used GitHub, which enabled version control through branches and pull requests. This allowed us to safely integrate changes, avoid conflicts, and maintain a stable and organized codebase.
-For communication, we used Messenger to quickly share updates, discuss ideas, and resolve issues in real time. This ensured that everyone stayed synchronized despite working on different parts of the project.
-Overall, these collaboration tools strengthened our teamwork, improved coordination, and helped us maintain a smooth and efficient development workflow.

## Slide 12 — Comclusion
**Presenter:** Pablo | **Duration:** 1 minute

-To conclude, this project was a successful experience where we designed and developed a functional AI-driven solution.
-One of our biggest takeaways is that AI systems are only as reliable as the data they are built upon. We learned how Retrieval-Augmented Generation, or RAG, helps ground AI responses in real data, while MCP enables different AI components to work together effectively.
-Through this project, we explored the concept of digital twins by creating data-driven representations capable of adapting and improving over time.
-From a teamwork perspective, we strengthened our collaboration, version control, and task management skills through tools such as ClickUp and GitHub.
-Moving forward, there are still areas for improvement, including creating a more user-friendly interface for non-technical users, adding automatic tailoring for different job postings, developing a stronger feedback loop, and enabling multi-candidate comparison.
-Overall, this project enhanced both our technical and collaborative skills and provided hands-on experience with modern AI systems, further improving our employability in the industry.
