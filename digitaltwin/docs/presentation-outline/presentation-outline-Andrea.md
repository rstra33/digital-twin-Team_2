# Slide 3 - Introduction

For this project, instead of just acting like a simple resume, our digital twin is designed to answer questions about us accurately, especially in interview scenarios. It uses real data from our history, making sure that the responses are based on facts and not generated randomly.

This is made possible through:

1. **Retrieval-Augmented Generation or RAG**, where the system retrieves relevant information from a database and uses it to generate accurate answers instead of relying on guesses.

2. We also implemented an **MCP Server**, which allows our system to be accessed and used by different AI agents.

3. The **system is cloud deployed using Vercel** which makes it accessible online anytime and anywhere.

Overall, what we built is not just a chatbot, but a strong foundation for future AI applications that can represent us professionally and accurately.

Let us proceed to our demo video, where  we will show how our AI digital twin works in an actual interview scenario — specifically how it responds to questions using our real data.
